01 — Problem

Industrial training
is broken

Workers master complex machines using paper manuals, word-of-mouth, and classroom sessions disconnected from real work.

📋
Static Manuals

Workers learn from PDFs and printouts that can't respond to real machine situations. Information is outdated and impossible to apply on-site.

⏳
Slow Onboarding

New hires shadow experienced workers for weeks. When those workers leave, critical institutional knowledge disappears with them.

⚠️
Safety Risks

Mistakes happen when workers face machine problems without real-time guidance. Training gaps lead to incidents, downtime, and liability.

📊
Zero Visibility

Managers have no data on worker skill levels, training completion, or where errors occur. Every decision is made blind.

02 — Solution

Training that lives
on the machine

TrainLab is a no-code SaaS platform that layers AR guidance directly onto real industrial equipment — via any smartphone or AR glasses.

📱
Point. See. Learn.

Workers point their phone at a machine and instantly see step-by-step AR instructions overlaid at the exact physical location — no QR codes, no app switching.

🎛️
No-Code Content Studio

Managers place AR hotspots, write instructions, and publish training modules from a web dashboard — no 3D modeling, no developer required.

🤖
AI-Assisted Creation

Upload a PDF manual and AI automatically converts it into structured AR step sequences — reducing setup time from days to hours.

📈
Real-Time Analytics

Track every step a worker completes, how long it takes, and where errors occur. Invisible training becomes measurable operational data.
